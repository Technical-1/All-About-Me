About Me

In the Fall of 2024, I graduated from the University of Florida with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering. I repaired my first phone when I was 12 and built my first computer when I was 13, leading me to a future in technology. Shortly after, I started teaching myself how to code and haven't looked back since.

During my time at UF, I gained hands-on consulting experience as a Data Science Intern at World Wide Technology, working on ML/AI initiatives to drive business decisions. I also held multiple leadership positions in student government and campus organizations, managing budgets exceeding $23 million and leading teams of 150+ members.

Since August 2025, I have been working at Deloitte as an Engineering Solutions Analyst in their Government & Public Services practice, within the AI & Data offering portfolio. Based in Arlington, VA, I specialize in designing and deploying AI-powered solutions and intelligent agents that solve complex challenges for public sector clients, enhancing operational efficiency and driving innovation through cutting-edge technology.
